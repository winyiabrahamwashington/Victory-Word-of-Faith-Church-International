About Our Church

"For with God nothing shall be impossible." - Luke 1:37

Victory Word of Faith Church International is a vibrant Pentecostal community dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ with power and demonstration of the Holy Spirit. Founded in 2005, we have grown from a small Bible study group to a thriving international ministry.

Our mission is to equip believers with the Word of God, empower them through the baptism of the Holy Spirit, and send them out to make disciples of all nations. We believe in the full operation of the gifts of the Spirit and the manifestation of God's power in our daily lives.

At Victory Word of Faith, you'll experience passionate worship, sound biblical teaching, and a loving community that will support you in your spiritual journey. Whether you're new to faith or a seasoned believer, there's a place for you here.

Our Leadership

"And he gave some, apostles; and some, prophets; and some, evangelists; and some, pastors and teachers..." - Ephesians 4:11

Pastor Dorothy Binwe

Senior Pastor & Founder

With over 25 years of ministry experience, Pastor Dorothy Binwe has a passion for teaching God's Word with clarity and power. She holds a Doctorate in Theology from Faith Bible Institute and has ministered in over 30 nations.

Pastor Dorothy founded Victory Word of Faith Church International in 2005 with a vision to raise up believers who walk in faith, power, and victory. Her dynamic preaching and prophetic ministry have touched thousands of lives.
